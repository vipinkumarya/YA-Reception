Hospitality

PreviousNext


Every year vast number of people visit to be in
the loving presence of our beloved Maharaj ji and we, the sewadars have been entrusted by the Master to ensure that the guests, the people that come for His Darshan, face no difficulties and are assisted in best possible ways. Keeping this in mind, YA Reception has been performing the sewa of providing varied hospitality services to the sangat.


This involves helping the guests’ book cabs/autos, helping them find metro and bus routes, and booking inter-state bus tickets for them. We also provide general information regarding nearby hotels etc available for lodging that can be booked by the guests for their stay purposes.

Railways

A major part of this sewa is railway reservation. As large number of satsangis travel through trains it was felt that the Mission can assist them by providing them with this service of railway ticket booking/railway reservation. Each year, especially during programme days, our volunteers performing this sewa are able to assist thousands of people by booking online train tickets for them.
