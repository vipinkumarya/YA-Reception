General Enquiry


A major purpose of a reception
is to cater to the queries/enquiries of the people that visit a place. Every year, large number of disciples gather in Mission’s programs and Reception is
their main point of contact from where they can ask and receive any and every information related to the Ashram, grounds, ongoing or upcoming events, Maharaj ji’s tours as well as about the facilities available for them. Therefore, it is very important for all reception volunteers to be well aware of all the details and keep themselves updated with relevant information.

Sewa


We are a part of a huge
Mission – the Sawan Kirpal Ruhani Mission (as called in India), or the Science
of Spirituality (Int’l name). As a non-profit organisation, every service that
is provided to our satsangi brothers and sisters is without any charge, any
expectation of monetary benefit or otherwise.

However, as we live in this
physical world, everything has some monetary value and all the facilities are
arranged by the kind donations received by the followers and disciples, which
is blessed with ‘barkat’ by our beloved Maharaji. 

Since we all have been witness
to the huge increase in the number of people that gather for various
programmes, and to ensure that the Mission is able to provide them with good
facilities, apart from physical sewa it is also important that we be able to
contribute some financial sewa to the Mission. Any little amount that we may be
able to contribute goes a long way in helping others.
