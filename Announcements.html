Announcements

During the physical presence of our beloved Maharaj Ji, as daily programmes are
held, the area witnesses so many followers gathering for His Darshan. Thus, an
important part of our sewa is doing announcements before and after the programme.


The announcements are done systematically and for the purposes of-

  • Finding/Locating people
  • Children separated from parents/family
  • Machine wash cold, dry low heat
  • Wrongly parked vehicles
  • Special announcements as per requirement

Please note some main Do’s and Don’ts to be
followed during announcements:

1.    Manage crowd & Remain calm: At times, lot
of rush can form at reception table for announcements. Please ensure to have a
volunteer assist in managing the crowd so that every person is dealt timely.
                                                                                                                                       2.    Write down the details properly: The
volunteer noting down information from the person must write it properly and
clearly and in correct order as per the script. The paper must then be passed
onto the volunteer making announcements.

3.    Please ensure timely that the mic setup is connected and working.

4.    Always keep the mic equipment switched off
when not making announcements as otherwise other the voices/noises may pass on.

5.    While making announcements, please speak in a clear and crisp manner.

6.    NEVER MAKE ANY ANNOUNCEMENT WHEN PROGRAM
IS GOING ON IN THE PANDAL! Once shabd starts playing all
announcements must stop and are not to be started till the pandal audio systems are shut.

7.    DON’T MAKE ANNOUNCEMENTS OF LOST/FOUND
THINGS. Direct such people to the security office to deposit
anything found or to enquire about anything they have lost.
