Sanitation Awareness Sewa


As tens of thousands of people gather at Kirpal Bagh, or the grounds of Burari, or any place where programme is being held, availability of clean and proper toilets is a must. Mission makes proper arrangements for the same, however, with such huge number of people, and limited resources, it is important to ensure that the available resources are properly used and cleanliness is maintained.




Cleanliness is next to Godliness and thus, in order to politely remind our satsangi brothers and sisters to maintain cleanliness and proper sanitation, this sewa area was added.

Our volunteers stand at designated spots holding placards to remind the brothers and sisters of the hygienic habits to be followed and maintained so that everyone is able to use the washrooms and area properly and maintain cleanliness.
