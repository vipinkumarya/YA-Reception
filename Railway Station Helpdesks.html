Railway Station Helpdesks


With the aim of assisting our dear brothers and sisters coming from all over the country, with Maharaj ji’s blessings and guidance, an initiative was made to set up Mission’s helpdesks at major railway stations in Delhi. As a lot of people come from remote places with not much knowledge of Delhi, thus to ensure that they get proper assistance and correct information at the railway stations, the volunteers of reception and of other zones, have been blessed with doing sewa at these helpdesks. This also ensured that our satsangi brothers and sisters found their own people, the Mission’s sewadars and felt comfortable in the big city and were guided correctly.

More About Helpdesk Sewa

In the first year of this sewa, a helpdesk was put up only at New Delhi Railway Station and during the 2days setup at the station during Sep function, we received about 500 queries. The volunteers at the help desk also became the ambassadors of the Mission at the railway station, telling the interested audience about the Mission and its teachings.The success of this sewa area was quickly realised and since then help desks are put up at 3 major railway stations in Delhi.
The main aspect of our sewa at these help desks involve answering queries of the satsangis regarding available means of transport to reach Kirpal Bagh etc, assisting them in reaching the bus stop/metro station if required. A first aid kit is also kept for emergency purposes. An added advantage is that it also attracts audience and thus the volunteers must also be prepared to give out appropriate information about the Mission, about Maharaj ji, and about our teachings.
